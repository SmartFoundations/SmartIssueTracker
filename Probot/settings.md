Smart! v22+ includes a dedicated settings panel in-game.

--Where To Find It--

1. Open the **Main Menu**
2. Navigate to **Settings > Mods > Smart!**

--Current Settings--

- **HUD Overlay:** Toggle on/off, adjust detail level
- **Direction Arrows:** Toggle axis arrow visuals while scaling
- **Keybinds:** Configure in **Options > Keybindings > Smart! Scaling Controls** (native bindings)

--Coming Soon--

- Auto-Connect options (enable/disable suggestions, behavior tweaks) once Phase 2 releases
- Additional HUD customization (placement, opacity, presets)
